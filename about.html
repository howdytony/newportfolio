Welcome to my portfolio! My approach to User Experience is a wholistic one—where branding and storytelling stands shoulder-to-shoulder with business needs and ease of use.

I cut my teeth teaching digital media art to middle schoolers in Chicago, honing research and presentation skills that now inform playful, education-driven interfaces.

Most recently, I teamed up with Gethsemane Garden Center—a beloved Chicago institution since 1977—to overhaul their visual identity and rebuild their website, crafting cohesive branding that resonates online, across social channels, and in the garden aisles, all while honoring their community-centered ethos.
